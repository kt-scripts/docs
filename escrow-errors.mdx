If you've just purchased and dropped a KT Scripts resource into your server, you may encounter one of the following escrow errors. This page covers the most common ones.

<Warning>
  Make sure your **CFX account email** matches the one you used at checkout, and that the resource is **whitelisted** on your CFX keymaster.
</Warning>

## Common errors

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="This resource is locked. (cfx-escrow)" icon="lock">
    The resource isn't yet linked to this server's CFX key.

    1. Open [keymaster.fivem.net](https://keymaster.fivem.net).
    2. Go to **My Assets** and find the script.
    3. Click **Manage** and add your **server key** under "Allowed CFX keys".
    4. Restart the resource.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Failed to verify protected resource" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    The escrow service couldn't be reached.

    - Confirm your server has outbound internet access on port 443.
    - Check the [FiveM status page](https://status.cfx.re).
    - Restart your server and try again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Resource manifest failed to load" icon="file-circle-xmark">
    Usually caused by a corrupted download.

    - Re-download from your CFX account.
    - Verify the folder name **exactly** matches the original (`kt_weed`, `kt_meth`, etc.).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
